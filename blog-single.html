MySchool Details

MySchool is website having many cool functionalities.

Nowadays, reforms related to education is being implemented. Therefore, MySchool can be one factor in improving our education system. Within it, you can see your child's ranking and their teacher's ranking also. You can also apply online mode and see which type of schools are better for you.

Website has 7-8 sections: School, Online apply, Subjects, Contact, About Us, Login and Register sections. The first section, you can any informations about 4 type of schools: President, Goverment schools and Special and Private schools.

Online apply sections provide you opportunity in order to apply schools via online mode. You don't need to go school wasting you precious time.And also Subjects section you can download any subjects e-version.
